Calculate Your Debt and Net Salary

Knowing your income and deducting expenses are the first steps in calculating your debt and net salary. To establish your net salary, first prove your gross salary. From there, subtract liabilities such as loans and credit card debt. Being aware of your debt-to-income ratio is essential for long-term financial security. Effective money management and future planning depend on saving and creating a budget. Maintaining a vision of your debt and net pay will help you move toward a more stable financial situation.
